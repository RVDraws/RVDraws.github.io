Summary

I am an aspiring artist who mainly focuses on superheroes, supervillains, and the occasional creatures. My art style was inspired by Popcross Studios, Marvel, DC, some anime, and many other artists. My characters are a mix between being based on real people, people I've made up, or a mix of characters. I have be drawing for as long as I can remember my current style, as stated before, was heavily inspired by Popcross Studios, I'd found Popcross Studios towards the end of 2020 and turned the break of art I said I was on. The characters I create are used within stories I've thought of and or currently writing stories for. These drawings normally take me about an hour to just under three hours to complete.

Goals

My goal as an artist is to be able to work on my characters and stories while improving my styles and learning different ones. As well as improve on my writing and how characters and scenes are written. If I was given the opportunity to work for character designs or background concept I'm not hesitating at that chance to improve and push my art out even more.
