Stories

Rory Vanson: The Demon Summoner

Rory Vanson: The Demon Summoner follows Rory who gets signs of an attachment by the Shadow Wolf who trains Rory to fight for him in a tournament
for the spot of The Devils right hand man. After some time training Rory and a group of other summoners work together to bring down a powerful spirt. Those in this world
who have spiritual powers or spend time around it can see Demons, Angel, Yakoi, Onis, Gods, spirits, and other spiritual entities. Rory later would be transferred to a school build for
heroes but not many knew it's true purpuse. At this school Rory would find his true powers and become a powerful being in his universe.

Monster Town

Monster Town is a series that will follow a group of six high schoolers that live in a town that every fourty-years monsters appear and kill residents. The high schoolers
face the first monster in fourty-years which happens to be a Axe Murderer. Every monster after the Axe Murderer is stranger and more daring as the stories go on.

God Love

Imagine a mythical world with not many gods still alive and monsters within the forrest human dare not touch and armies made of a mix of greek, egyptian, and a small hit of norse warriors.
God Love follows the child of the God of Lighting and War and the God of the Universe and Art. This child traveled months to a new land were he could try and find a wife
just as strong as him and does. Though she doesn't become his wife the god he finds gives him a chance, after seeing what made him the champion of his land and learning she can't go long
without energy from her realm.
