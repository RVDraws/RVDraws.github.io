Artwork

This artwork is of Ren Willson who can summon four elemental demons: Land, Air, Water, and Fire. Ren can either summon the demons to fight for him or he can summon parts of the demons onto his body, like
demon summoners. The artwork above is Ren summoning all four demons at once. Ren's powers developed from a young age and the scar on his face was
giving to him by the Land demon that he had summoned at young age, as well as summoned at all the time.

The Black Mane come from the same world as Ren Willson and Rory Vanson but years in the future in the story "Next Gen". Black Mane was inspired by
Marvels Kingpin and the artist, on instagram, oni8064's Empire Penguin. Black Mane is the leader of the Black Lions and within these Black Lions is a thing called "Addicts" where they put
monster blood in their blood. Black Mane's addict form was inpired by DC Bane and uses Chinese Guard Lion for the Blood.

Within this peice shows a group of demon summoners fighting against a demon overlord. The drawings takes place in the later part of Rory Vanson: The Demon Summoner. The summoners are from different
parts of the world and use different styles of summoning. One of the summoners is Ren from the image above, one of the other summoners use music to summon skulls, as shown, another is a psychic inspired by
Momo Ayase from DanDaDan. The two other summoners are Maddie, Rory's friend, who can summon a Yokai and a oni summoner, whos injured, and who uses a mask to summon the Oni.
The final character in this drawing is a demon hurter inspired by JJK sorcerers, one character whos not in this peice is an Angel summoner.
